About us

Welcome to RideAlong

Welcome to Ride Along, your premier bike rental service in the heart of Gangtok, Sikkim. Established in March 2022 and located in the bustling area of Sisha Golai, we are dedicated to providing adventurers and tourists with a unique and exhilarating way to explore the breathtaking landscapes of Sikkim.

At Ride Along, we offer a wide range of well-maintained bikes suitable for every type of rider, from solo travelers to groups looking to explore together. Whether you're an experienced rider or a beginner, our diverse fleet ensures that you'll find the perfect bike for your journey.
